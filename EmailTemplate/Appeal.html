Dear Esteemed Seniors, Teachers, and Friends,

Dated: 14.09.2024

Hon’ble Justice J.R. Midha (Retd), Administrators,
Indian Orthopaedic Association, New Delhi.

Subject: Elections 2023

Dear Sir,
IOA has seen tumultuous times since the elections held in 2020. A drama unfolded which could make excellent material for a television series. Thankfully, perseverance has prevailed, and the Hon’ble High Court has now declared the 2020 elections nullified and vitiated. The court also ruled against the restitution of the Election Committee (EC) that declared the 2020 results.

In this context, IOA now stands at a crossroads where we have an opportunity to reward those who have fought for justice. The most significant contribution members can make to IOA today is to ensure the election of two esteemed candidates unopposed. This will encourage other like-minded and visionary individuals to step forward and lead IOA into a bright future.

I request you all to please support the candidature of Dr. PV Vijayraghavan for Vice President and Dr. Manish Dhawan for Secretary General of IOA. This will be a befitting tribute to their crusade to cleanse IOA for the better.

Their credentials are already well-known, so I do not need to elaborate. By electing them, you will not only restore IOA’s integrity but also save the association the financial burden of another drawn-out election process.

In case the results are otherwise, I can assure you that IOA will never ever recover its pristine glory and aura, which our founding fathers dreamed of.

In conclusion, I earnestly pray for IOA’s deliverance and look forward to a new beginning.

Thanking you in anticipation,

Atul Bahadur Singh
